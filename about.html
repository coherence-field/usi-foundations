About

The USI Foundations define a multi‑layer symbolic architecture for constructing, transforming, and integrating coherent structures across systems. Each layer—COP, USI, Generative, CAGS, and Integration—contributes a distinct set of operators, invariants, and state geometries that together form a unified generative framework.

The project is organized to maintain clarity, altitude, and structural consistency. The foundational papers establish the canonical definitions. The reference diagrams express the geometry of each layer. The reference sequences express the dynamics of coherent motion within and across layers.

The aim is to provide a stable, transmissible foundation for symbolic cognition and cross‑architecture coherence, independent of domain or implementation.
